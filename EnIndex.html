About Us

“

"The journey from sleepless nights saving deadlines..."

Have you ever felt your heart 'tighten' when your laptop suddenly Blue Screens right when your project is due in just a few hours?

We – IT students at Thai Nguyen University – have been in that exact position. TechSupport didn't start from grand business plans; it began from sleepless nights helping roommates recover data and afternoons spent cleaning laptops for friends in the dormitory.

For us, no student should have to pause their creative journey or miss a learning opportunity due to avoidable computer mishaps. TechSupport doesn't just sell repair services; we provide Peace of Mind and Partnership.

Whether it's a tricky software error at midnight or deep hardware upgrade needs, our team is ready to support you directly at your rental room or dormitory. The "Student-to-Student" philosophy is our North Star, helping us understand your pressure better than anyone else.

At TechSupport, you don't just find technicians; you find reliable technology companions.
