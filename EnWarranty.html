Warranty & Liability Policy

Commitment to transparency - Dedicated support for the student community.

1. Warranty Period & Scope

  • Software Warranty: 07-day support commitment from the date of device handover. If any conflicts or missing installations occur, our team will re-process them entirely free of charge.
  • Maintenance Services: Guaranteed stable temperatures and quiet fan operation for the first 03 days.
  • Hardware: Our team provides upgrade consulting (RAM/SSD) at cost price. Hardware warranties follow the supplier's policy (typically 12-36 months).

2. The Golden Rule: Data

Data is a student's most valuable asset. TechSupport applies strict data rules:

  • Customers are responsible for backing up important data before handing over their devices.
  • Our team is not liable for any data loss during OS reinstallation or hardware intervention.
Please always back up to the Cloud or a USB drive before any repairs!

3. Warranty Exclusions

We cannot provide free warranty support in the following cases:

  • Our warranty seal is torn, erased, or shows signs of being tampered with/opened.
  • The device has been dropped, suffered water damage, or physical impact after handover.
  • The user has performed deep system interventions or installed unsafe "cracked" software leading to errors.

4. Our Commitment

For severe hardware issues (Mainboard failure, screen damage, explosions), we will honestly advise you to visit official service centers (Dell, HP, Apple, etc.) rather than attempting a temporary fix to avoid further damage.
